Condo Project Summary
A new master-planned community collection is coming animate Markham’s growing downtown corridor. UnionCity Condos is a collection of modern condominium by Metropia in Markham. This exclusive set of buildings will be located near the vibrant intersection of Enterprise Boulevard & Birchmount Road in Unionville. This pre-construction project will feature four new residential buildings with varying heights; and up to 1,400 new units. This location will offer convenient access to world-class amenities, schools, recreation and neighbourhood services.
Modern, but inspired by historical Unionville. UnionCity is coming to Markham and will surely redefine luxury living. A master-planned city that will seamlessly mesh living, recreation and all needed amenities together.
With stunning design, both on the exterior and interior of the residences, UnionCity promises to be a landmark area in Markham.

Location & Neighbourhood
UnionCity Condos will be arriving in Markham’s downtown neighbourhood, also known Unionville. This region is an excellent location for people of all ages due to its proximity to excellent amenities. This area is home to Markham’s entertainment hub and transit hub making it a great place to live and invest in.
The best part of being in the downtown area is its proximity to family-friendly urban amenities. These include a GoodLife Fitness, banks, Cineplex Cinemas Markham and VIP, and a diverse range of restaurants and speciality shops which can be found within walking distance. Downtown Markham is a 243​-acre community budding with diverse, multi-lingual, highly educated, well-employed and incredibly trend-conscious members.
